FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Have questions? We have answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out to us.

What is online therapy and how does it work? +
Online therapy connects you with a licensed mental health professional via video, phone, or messaging. Sessions are conducted in a private, secure environment — just like traditional therapy but from the comfort of your home.
Is my information confidential? +
Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All sessions are encrypted end-to-end and your personal information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
How do I know which therapist is right for me? +
We use a detailed intake assessment to match you with specialists who align with your goals, preferences, and concerns. You can also browse profiles, read reviews, and switch therapists at any time.
Can I cancel or reschedule my session? +
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your session at no charge. We understand life happens and we aim to be as flexible as possible.
Does insurance cover online therapy? +
Many insurance providers now cover online therapy. We work with several major insurers and can provide a superbill for reimbursement. Check with your provider for specific coverage details.
